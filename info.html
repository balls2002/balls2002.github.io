Other Juggling Societies and Events

londonjuggling.org lists juggling events in and around London.

Here are other juggling societies in UK universities. They'll have their own links and general information, which might be useful. Some of these may no longer exist since the list was last updated in 2004 or never came back since the pandemic, but most of them seem kicking and alive.

  • Aberdeen Circus Society
  • Bath Gravity Vomit
  • Birmingham Circus Society
  • Cambridge Juggler's Association
  • Edinburgh Magic & Circus Society
  • Lancaster Magic & Circus Society
  
  • Lincoln Juggling Society
  • Leeds Juggle Soc
  • Sheffield Flying Teapots Society
  • Southampton Circus Society
  • York Circus & Magic Society

The open directory dmoz maintains lists of Juggling Clubs and Organizations and College Juggling Clubs. Many of them are in the states.
