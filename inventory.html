Equipment and Loans

The Society own a large amount of juggling equipment - see our inventory below for more details. We don't usually bring out all of the kit every week just because of the sheer amount of stuff we would have to carry, but we endavour to have at least one of each kind of equipment avaliable. Let us know in advance via WhatsApp or any of our socials if there is any particular kit you would like to use during the session and we could bring it out of the store room.

Most equipment are also avaliable for loans by members to practice with outside of our weekly sessions, subject to terms and conditions below. To borrow an item of equipment, speak to a member of the Committee at our regular Tuesday sessions.

Terms and Conditions

    1. Equipment may only be borrowed by paid members. Such loans are free, provided these rules are adhered to.
    2. Any equipment borrowed must be signed for in the Equipment Loans Book, and returned to a Committee member at the next weekly session.
    3. Equipment loans may be extended on request at the discretion of the Committee. cf. §3(d) of the Code of Conduct.
    4. Neither the Society nor Students' Union UCL shall take any responsibility for any loss, damage, or injury caused by, or in connection with, the use of the loaned equipment.
    5. Normal wear and tear is expected, and quite acceptable. However, members are expected to pay for any loss or damage resulting from misuse or negligence.
    6. By signing the Equipment Loans Book, the member agrees to these terms and conditions.

Wholesale Orders

We aim to put a wholesale order of juggling equipment once a term via JWS Europe at discounted prices. Ask a member of the Committee to enquire about the prices. If you absolutely cannot wait to buy some of your own kit, you may want to visit Oddballs' retail outlet in Camden Market.
