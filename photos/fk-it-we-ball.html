Album — F**k It We Ball: the 20th Anniversary Variety Show

The Society celebrated its 20th anniversary with an entire evening of entire evening of performances including a fire show on the Main Quad and a variety show in Mully's, starring opening acts from UCL Graters, Comedy Club, and Magic Society. We were also very honoured to be able to invite some of the founding members of the society, including Cameron, Patrick, and others. Here are some highlights from the evening; you can see the rest of the pictures here.

31/01/2023 — Second Half @ Mully's

For the second half, we hosted a variety show in Mully's Bar with an audience of about 80 people. We kicked it off with three brilliant opening acts from the UCL Graters, Comedy Club, and Magic Society, followed by a 10-minute stand up routine by Marian. We then had two intellectually stimulating PowerPoint presentations by Seth and Marco about the political opinions of Madagascar characters and conspiracy theories respectively before the main act of the evening, JACS and the Holy G(RA)il, a play we wrote about our journey in securing the fire juggling risk assessment. Seth rounded off the night with a Kahoot quiz, in which the winner was awarded a t-shirt and the first and second runner ups were awarded a silver and a black bin respectively.

31/01/2023 — First Half @ Main Quad

We kicked off the evening in the Main Quad with an amazing fire show by (in order of appearance) Lorik, Romy, Finn, Maddie, Shuji, Justin, and Marlin. Many thanks to Tianming for these absolutely incredible photos. Check out his Instagram here.
